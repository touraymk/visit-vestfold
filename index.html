why vestfold?

Vestfold, explore the Viking trails

Vestfold is the place in Norway boasting the most traces of our proud Viking heritage. The region between Mølen in the south and Borre in the north has many places worth visiting. Over a distance of 60 kilometres(40 miles) you will discover large, significant burial mounds, remains of Norway's first town and where the most important finds from the Viking age were made. Vestfoldeven has its own Viking ships, both original and replicas.
